We collect information necessary to provide our services, including:

  • Account information (email, username, verification documents)
  • Transaction history and allocation records
  • Technical data (IP addresses, device information, browser type)
  • Usage data (platform interactions, feature usage)
  • Communication records (support tickets, emails)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process transactions and allocations
  • To ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • To communicate important updates and information
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

Important Security Note

We never store your private keys or wallet passwords. All cryptographic operations are performed client-side. We implement industry-standard security measures including end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits.

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Transaction records are maintained for a minimum of 7 years to comply with financial regulations.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability

Compliance & Security

Our commitment to regulatory compliance and security

Compliance Framework

We maintain a comprehensive compliance program that includes:

KYC/AML Procedures
Mandatory identity verification for all users, continuous transaction monitoring, and suspicious activity reporting.
Risk Assessment
Regular risk assessments, vulnerability testing, and third-party security audits conducted quarterly.

Security Measures

  • Encryption: End-to-end AES-256 encryption for all data transmissions
  • Wallet Security: Multi-signature wallets with cold storage for majority of assets
